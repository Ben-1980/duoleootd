About Duole

关于多樂

Founded in 2023, Duole is a game development company that specializes in mobile entertainment. Our diverse portfolio includes popular titles such as "Immortal Diaries", "Palace Rule", and "Victorian Clue Hunter". At Duole, we strive to push the boundaries of innovation and creativity to deliver female-oriented games that exceed expectations.

多樂信息技術有限公司成立于2023年，是一家专注于移动娱乐的游戏开发公司。我们拥有多样化的产品组合，包括《永生日记》、《宫廷法则》和《维多利亚线索猎人》等热门游戏。在多樂，我们致力于突破创新与创意的边界，提供超越期待的女性向游戏体验。
